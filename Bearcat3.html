Bearcat III PT 300

Specs

  • Model: Bearcat III PT 300
  • Year: 1978
  • Engine: Caterpillar 3306
  • Horsepower: ~300 HP
  • Drive: 4WD
  • Tires: 18.4×38

This is our Steiger Bearcat III PT 300. The original 3306 had electrolysis issues, so we repowered it with a 300 HP Caterpillar 3306 from a Freightliner truck. It’s a strong, capable tractor that fits right in with the work we do. Like the rest of our equipment, it’s something we enjoy running and learning from. These older Steigers were built tough, and this one continues to prove why they’re still respected machines today.
