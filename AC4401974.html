1974 Allis-Chalmers 440

Specs

  • Model: Allis-Chalmers 440
  • Year: 1974
  • Engine: Caterpillar 3208
  • Horsepower: 160
  • Drive: 4WD
  • Tires: 23.1×30

This is our 1974 Allis-Chalmers 440. After the Cummins 555 got water in the oil, we repowered it with a Caterpillar 3208 from a Cat grader scraper. It doesn’t have a ton of power, but it gets the job done and works well for what we use it for.
