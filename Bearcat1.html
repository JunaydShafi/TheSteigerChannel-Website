Bearcat I ST 250

Specs

  • Model: Bearcat I ST 250
  • Year: 1972
  • Engine: Caterpillar 3208 (Turbocharged)
  • Horsepower: ~250 HP
  • Drive: 4WD
  • Tires: 23.1×34

This is our Steiger Bearcat I that has been painted up to resemble a Series II. It has been repowered with a Caterpillar 3208 engine and a turbo, bringing it to around 250 horsepower. The tractor is currently running 23.1×34 tires and has proven to be a solid, reliable machine for us. Overall, it’s a great tractor that has treated us well and hasn’t given us any major issues.
