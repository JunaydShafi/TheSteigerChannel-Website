Cougar III PT 280

Specs

  • Model: Cougar III PT 280
  • Year: 1980
  • Engine: Cummins M11
  • Horsepower: 280 HP
  • Drive: 4WD
  • Tires: 23.1×34 Radial

This is our Steiger Cougar III PT 280. It was originally a Bearcat III that was used heavily in laser scraping applications, but it was later repowered with a Cummins M11 when it was sold to a dealership and had a lot of work done to it. It now has a new transfer box and transmission, and the engine is brand new as well. It’s a good, honest tractor that gets the job done without a lot of fuss. We enjoy running it and learning what it’s best at. It’s another example of why these machines still have a place on the farm today.
