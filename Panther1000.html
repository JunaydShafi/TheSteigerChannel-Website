Panther 1000 KP 1325

Specs

  • Model: Panther 1000 KP 1325
  • Year: 1982
  • Engine: Cummins 855
  • Horsepower: 325
  • Drive: 4WD
  • Tires: 23.1×34 Radial

This is our Panther 1000 KP 1325. It’s a serious piece of equipment and a big part of what makes this hobby fun for us — not because it’s perfect, but because keeping it running teaches us a lot. We’re grateful to have it and we’re happy to share what we learn along the way. It has a CP-1360 tilt hood because our flip up hood was damaged in a storm.
