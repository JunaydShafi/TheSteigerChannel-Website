1973 Allis-Chalmers 440

Specs

  • Model: Allis-Chalmers 440
  • Year: 1973
  • Engine: Cummins 555
  • Horsepower: 225
  • Drive: 4WD
  • Tires: 23.1×30

This is our 1973 Allis-Chalmers 440. It’s a classic tractor that we enjoy for what it is — simple, tough, and full of character. We’re not experts, but we enjoy keeping old machines running and sharing what we learn along the way. It currently has a repowered Cummins 555 from a cement truck with unknown hours, and we’re planning to put an 8.3 Cummins into it in the future.
